SEO Questions Answered

Everything Sydney business owners ask about SEO services

How long does SEO take to show results?

For local SEO, you can expect to see initial improvements within 30-60 days, with significant results in 3-6 months. National SEO typically takes 6-12 months for competitive keywords. We provide monthly reports so you can track progress every step of the way.

What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?

SEO is organic (free) search results that build long-term value, while Google Ads are paid advertisements. SEO takes longer but provides lasting results and higher trust. Google Ads give immediate visibility but stop when you stop paying. We recommend both for maximum impact.

Do you guarantee first page rankings?

We guarantee significant ranking improvements within 60 days or your money back. While no ethical SEO company can guarantee specific rankings (Google's algorithm changes constantly), we have a 95% success rate getting clients to page 1 for their target keywords.

How is local SEO different from regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on "near me" searches and location-specific keywords. It includes Google My Business optimization, local citations, and suburb-specific content. It's perfect for businesses serving Sydney customers physically (like plumbers, dentists, restaurants).

Can you help improve my Google My Business listing?

Absolutely! Google My Business optimization is a core part of our local SEO service. We optimize your profile, manage reviews, add posts, optimize photos, and ensure your business information is consistent across all platforms.

What SEO tools and software do you use?

We use enterprise-level tools including SEMrush, Ahrefs, Google Analytics, Google Search Console, Screaming Frog, and proprietary tracking software. You'll get access to detailed reports showing exactly how your SEO campaign is performing.

Do you work with any industry or business size?

We work with all types of Sydney businesses - from local service providers to e-commerce stores to professional services. Our packages scale from small local businesses ($1,500/month) to large enterprises ($4,500+/month). Every strategy is customized to your industry and goals.

What happens if I stop SEO services?

Your rankings will gradually decline over time as competitors continue optimizing and Google's algorithm evolves. However, the content we create and technical improvements remain. SEO is an ongoing investment, like maintaining a gym membership - you need consistency for long-term results.
