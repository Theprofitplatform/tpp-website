Google Ads FAQ

Common questions about Google Ads management and PPC advertising for Sydney businesses.

How quickly can my Google Ads campaigns go live?

Your Google Ads campaigns can be live within 24-48 hours after account setup. I'll need access to your Google Ads account (or create a new one), review your website, conduct keyword research, and set up the initial campaigns. Most campaigns start generating traffic immediately once approved by Google.

What's the minimum ad spend budget for Google Ads?

I recommend a minimum ad spend of $1,500-$2,000 per month for Search campaigns to gather meaningful data and compete effectively in Sydney's market. For Display and other campaign types, we can work with smaller budgets. Your total investment includes the ad spend plus my management fee.

How do you measure Google Ads success and ROI?

Success is measured by metrics that matter to your business: cost per lead, conversion rate, return on ad spend (ROAS), and ultimately, revenue generated. I track everything from click-through rates to actual sales, providing monthly reports that show exactly how your investment is performing.

Can you take over existing Google Ads campaigns?

Absolutely! I regularly take over underperforming campaigns and turn them profitable. I'll conduct a comprehensive audit of your existing account, identify issues, and implement improvements. Many clients see significant improvements within the first month of optimization.

Do you manage campaigns for businesses outside Sydney?

While I specialize in Sydney businesses and have deep local market knowledge, I work with clients across Australia. My Google Ads expertise applies anywhere, though Sydney businesses benefit from my additional local insights and understanding of the competitive landscape.

What happens if my campaigns don't perform well?

I'm committed to your success and continuously optimize campaigns for better performance. If campaigns aren't meeting expectations, I'll adjust strategies, test new approaches, and work closely with you to improve results. With no long-term contracts, you're never locked into poor performance.

How often do you optimize and adjust campaigns?

I monitor campaigns daily and make optimizations as needed. Minor adjustments happen weekly, while larger strategic changes are implemented bi-weekly or monthly based on performance data. You'll receive detailed monthly reports showing all changes and improvements made.

Do you provide Google Ads training or account handover?

Yes! If you decide to manage campaigns in-house later, I provide comprehensive training and documentation to ensure a smooth transition. You'll receive detailed explanations of all campaign setups, strategies used, and best practices for ongoing management.
