SEO Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common SEO questions from Sydney business owners. Still have questions? Contact us for a free consultation.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy that builds momentum over time. Most Sydney businesses start seeing improved rankings within 3-4 months, with significant traffic increases by month 6-12. Local SEO results can appear faster, often within 30-60 days for Google My Business improvements. The timeline depends on your competition, current website state, and chosen keywords.

What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?

SEO focuses on organic (unpaid) search results and builds long-term visibility, while Google Ads provides immediate paid placement. SEO takes longer but offers lasting results and lower cost per click over time. Google Ads gives instant visibility but requires ongoing payment. Most successful Sydney businesses use both strategies together for maximum impact.

Do you guarantee first page rankings?

No reputable SEO expert can guarantee specific rankings as Google's algorithm constantly changes and considers hundreds of factors. However, we do guarantee that we'll follow best practices, provide transparent reporting, and work tirelessly to improve your visibility. Our Sydney clients typically see significant improvements in rankings, traffic, and leads within 6 months.

What's included in your SEO audit?

Our comprehensive SEO audit includes: technical SEO analysis, current keyword rankings, competitor analysis, on-page optimization review, backlink profile assessment, Google My Business evaluation, site speed and mobile testing, and a custom strategy roadmap. This $997 audit is free for new Sydney clients and provides a clear action plan for improvement.

How do you handle local SEO for Sydney businesses?

Local SEO is our specialty! We optimize your Google My Business profile, build citations in Australian directories, create location-specific content, manage customer reviews, and implement schema markup for local businesses. We understand Sydney's competitive landscape and know which local signals Google values most for businesses in different suburbs.

Can you help with existing website SEO issues?

Absolutely! We specialize in fixing SEO problems on existing websites. Common issues include slow loading speeds, mobile compatibility problems, duplicate content, poor site structure, and penalty recovery. We'll audit your current site, identify all issues, and provide a prioritized fix list to get your Sydney business ranking again.
