1. Agreement Overview

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between The Profit Platform ("Service Provider") and the undersigned client ("Client") for the provision of digital marketing and business consulting services on a month-to-month basis.

2. Services Provided

The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services as selected and detailed in the accompanying Statement of Work (SOW):

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Google Ads Management (PPC)
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Website Design & Development
  • Conversion Rate Optimization
  • Content Marketing
  • Email Marketing
  • Marketing Analytics & Reporting
  • Business Strategy Consulting

3. Service Terms

3.1 Term

This Agreement operates on a month-to-month basis, automatically renewing each month unless terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

3.2 No Lock-in Contracts

The Client is not bound by any long-term commitment and may terminate services with 30 days notice without penalty.

3.3 Service Hours

Standard service hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM AEDT/AEST. Emergency support and urgent matters will be addressed as needed.

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Fees

Service fees are as specified in the Statement of Work and are billed monthly in advance.

4.2 Payment Schedule

  • Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month
  • Payment is due within 7 days of invoice date
  • Accepted payment methods: Bank transfer, credit card, or PayPal

4.3 Late Payments

Late payments may incur a fee of 2% per month on outstanding balances. Services may be suspended for accounts more than 30 days overdue.

5. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

  • Provide timely access to necessary accounts, platforms, and information
  • Review and approve materials within agreed timeframes
  • Maintain ownership and responsibility for business licenses and legal compliance
  • Provide accurate business information and marketing materials
  • Designate a primary point of contact for communications

6. Deliverables & Reporting

6.1 Monthly Reporting

The Service Provider will provide comprehensive monthly reports including:

  • Campaign performance metrics
  • ROI analysis
  • Recommendations for optimization
  • Upcoming strategy and initiatives

6.2 Communication

Regular communication includes:

  • Monthly strategy calls
  • Email updates as needed
  • Direct phone access to Avi for urgent matters

All existing Client materials remain the property of the Client.

7.2 Work Product

Upon full payment, all work product created specifically for the Client becomes the Client's property, except for proprietary methodologies and frameworks of the Service Provider.

7.3 Service Provider Tools

The Service Provider retains ownership of all proprietary tools, methodologies, and frameworks used in service delivery.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during the engagement. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

9. Performance Guarantees

9.1 Best Efforts

The Service Provider commits to using industry best practices and proven strategies to achieve Client objectives.

9.2 No Guarantee of Specific Results

While we strive for exceptional results, specific outcomes cannot be guaranteed due to factors beyond our control, including market conditions, competition, and platform algorithm changes.

10. Limitation of Liability

The Service Provider's liability is limited to the total fees paid by the Client in the preceding three months. The Service Provider is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

11. Termination

11.1 Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

11.2 Immediate Termination

Either party may terminate immediately for material breach that remains uncured after 10 days written notice.

11.3 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

  • All account access will be transferred to the Client
  • Final invoice for work completed will be issued
  • Client data and materials will be returned within 14 days

12. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will first be addressed through good faith negotiation. If unresolved, disputes will be settled through mediation in Sydney, NSW, Australia.

13.2 Amendments

Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

13.3 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia.

13.4 Severability
