üõ°Ô∏è Ethical Boundaries & Guidelines

‚ö†Ô∏è Never Use These Tactics
Fake Scarcity: Artificial timers that reset, fake "last item" claims, or made-up limitations
Emotional Manipulation: Fear-mongering, guilt-tripping, or exploiting insecurities
False Urgency: Evergreen timers, fake deadlines, or pressure without legitimate reason
Bait & Switch: Changing offers after engagement or adding hidden conditions
‚úÖ Ethical Guidelines Checklist
  1. Truthfulness: Every claim must be 100% accurate and verifiable
  2. Transparency: Explain the real reasons behind urgency or scarcity
  3. Value-First: Focus on helping customers make the right decision
  4. Respect: Honor all promises and deadlines without exception
  5. Alternatives: Always offer next steps if current offer doesn't suit
  6. Customer Benefit: Urgency should benefit the customer, not just the business

üìà Key Metrics to Track
  1. Conversion Rate: Compare urgency vs non-urgency CTAs
  2. Engagement Time: How long users spend with urgent messaging
  3. Bounce Rate: Ensure urgency doesn't increase bounces
  4. Customer Quality: Track LTV of urgency-driven customers
  5. Trust Metrics: Monitor reviews and satisfaction scores
  6. Completion Rate: From click to actual booking/purchase
